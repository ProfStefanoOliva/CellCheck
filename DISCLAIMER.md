# Disclaimer

CellCheck is a support tool for guided correction of spreadsheet exercises.

It does not replace the professional judgment of the teacher, examiner, or educational institution using it.

## Important limitations

- Results should always be reviewed by the teacher before being used in real grading workflows.
- The software does not guarantee perfect correction in every spreadsheet scenario.
- Excel formulas are not recalculated internally by CellCheck.
- `.xlsm` workbooks are handled in prudent read-only mode and macros are not executed.
- Original Excel workbooks are not intended to be modified or resaved by the application during standard analysis flows.

## User responsibility

The user remains responsible for the educational, evaluative, and administrative use of the software and for verifying whether generated results are appropriate in context.
