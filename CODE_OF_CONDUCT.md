# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

CellCheck aims to maintain a technical and educational community that is respectful, constructive, and professionally collaborative.

Participants are expected to:

- communicate respectfully;
- discuss technical disagreements in good faith;
- avoid harassment, hostility, or intimidation;
- respect privacy and sensitive educational data concerns;
- help keep the project welcoming to contributors with different backgrounds and experience levels.

## Unacceptable behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- personal attacks;
- insulting, hostile, or discriminatory language;
- deliberate misrepresentation of facts or authorship;
- publishing sensitive student, institutional, or personal data;
- disruptive conduct that prevents constructive project collaboration.

## Enforcement

Project maintainership may moderate discussions, close contributions, or restrict participation when behavior materially harms the project or its contributors.

## Reporting

Please report serious conduct concerns through the official repository channels unless a more specific contact method is later published.
