# Brand Guidelines

## Official identity

- Official product name: `CellCheck`
- Author/Maintainer: Stefano Oliva
- Official repository: [https://github.com/ProfStefanoOliva/CellCheck](https://github.com/ProfStefanoOliva/CellCheck)

## Correct use of the name

Allowed examples:

- `This project is based on CellCheck`
- `Unofficial fork of CellCheck`
- `Compatible with CellCheck profiles`

## Not allowed without authorization

- `CellCheck Professional`
- `CellCheck Official`
- Any product title that implies official endorsement, authorship, or release management by Stefano Oliva when that is not true

## Logo and icon

The official logo, icon, screenshots, and brand assets are reserved for the official project unless explicit permission is granted.

Do not use the official CellCheck logo or icon to distribute a modified version in a way that may confuse users about its origin.

## Visual theme

The official visual identity, including the dark theme and associated branding elements, is part of the recognized product presentation. Reuse in forks should avoid creating confusion with the official project unless explicit authorization is granted.

## Forks and modified versions

Forks are allowed under the open source code license terms intended for the project, but modified versions should:

- use a different product name;
- avoid official branding assets;
- clearly identify themselves as unofficial;
- avoid claims of endorsement by Stefano Oliva unless permission has been granted.

## Note

This document provides project governance guidance and is not definitive legal advice. For trademark registration, enforcement, commercial brand licensing, or other binding legal questions, consult a qualified legal professional.
